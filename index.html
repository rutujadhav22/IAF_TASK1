Building brighter futures through compassion, education, dignity, and action.

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit organization founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. Based in Chhattisgarh and active across India, the foundation works to support underserved communities through education, food and clothing drives, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental action, and youth skill development.

About The NGO

A grassroots organization focused on lasting social impact.

Why InAmigos exists

The foundation works to bridge gaps in opportunity and dignity. Its mission is built on inclusivity, empathy, collaboration, and community-led action across education, healthcare awareness, livelihoods, and sustainability.

How the work creates change

Instead of focusing on one issue alone, InAmigos runs multiple programs that respond to real community needs: feeding people and animals, supporting children, training women, planting trees, and preparing youth for work.

Why awareness matters

Awareness turns observers into volunteers, donors, advocates, and long-term supporters. Every shared story helps more people learn that social change becomes stronger when communities participate together.

Ongoing Projects

Six initiatives shaping education, welfare, empowerment, and sustainability.

Project SEVA

Food, clothing, and dignity

Supports underprivileged communities with meals, clothing, and basic relief while raising awareness around hunger and social inclusion.

Project Bachpanshala

Education for children

Helps underprivileged children through school support, digital literacy, life skills, workshops, mentoring, and safe learning spaces.

Project JEEV

Animal welfare

Feeds and protects stray animals, encourages humane care, and supports a culture of compassion for voiceless lives.

Project UDAAN

Women empowerment

Promotes skill development, digital literacy, entrepreneurship, leadership, and financial independence for women.

Project PRAKRITI

Environmental action

Encourages tree plantation, clean-up campaigns, conservation awareness, and eco-friendly habits for a greener future.

Project VIKAS

Youth employability

Connects students and young professionals with internship opportunities, career guidance, and practical skill-building.

Purpose Of The Projects

Each initiative solves a different social challenge, but all lead toward the same goal.

InAmigos Foundation’s projects are designed to create both immediate relief and long-term empowerment. SEVA addresses urgent needs like food and clothing. Bachpanshala works on educational opportunity. UDAAN strengthens women’s independence. JEEV protects neglected animals. PRAKRITI promotes environmental responsibility. VIKAS helps youth build employable skills and confidence. Together, these efforts aim to build a more inclusive, compassionate, and self-reliant society.
